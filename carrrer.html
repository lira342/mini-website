CAREER PATH

For me to become a software developer, i should:

Earn my education, I have to build a strong foundation in programming through a bootcamp, a degree or online courses. I have to gain experience, I have to look for freelance work, internships or small projects that will help me practice coding in real world situations. I have to build a portfolio that shows my best projects and also prove my skills to my future employers. I have to earn certifications in areas like software development and cloud computing like freecodecamp or codecademy certifications to be able to strengthen my skills and knowledge and make my resume stand out. I also create a simple resume that highlights my skills, projects and experience.
